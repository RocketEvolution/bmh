Company Overview


BMH Systems Solution Inc., was established and registered on May 27, 2014 as Sales Company that offers access of selling Cable TV, Professional and Broadcast Equipment. BMH also offers full service engineering that provides a complete turnkey solution integration, repair and maintenance for broadcasters and other telecommunication companies throughout the country. We provide everything from Design and Engineering, Consulting, Installation, Project Management, providing Technical Docs and VIDCAD to the broadcasting industry and provide workflow solutions for data-centric post and cable head end facilities.
