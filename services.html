BMH Services


BMH's services provide the following solutions as well as turnkey operations:

  • Project Management
  • Complete Systems Design
  • Equipment Sales, Supply and Delivery
  • Offsite Prefabrication
  • Onsite Systems Installation
  • Testing and commissioning
  • Engineering support
  • Custom Manufacturing
  • Design and Integration
  • Full Service and Support

The growth and development of BMH Systems Solution Inc. over the years has seen it evolve into a diverse and unique company that offers clients a complete package solution. BMH offers design, installation and maintenance services. From product sales and support, small or large full-scale designed and integrated systems, BMH upholds an important emphasis on client interaction, as well as communication across all stakeholders.

The core team at BMH combined have worked on countless projects. A lot of this business has been repeat customers whom as a result of their satisfaction with BMH's services, have continued to choose us. We have the experience and offer the service that continues to count with our clients.

Regardless of how large or small the project is, whether it’s simply equipment dry hire or a fully-integrated system build for broadcasters, BMH works seamlessly alongside the client to ensure the highest production standards are achieved for each project undertaken.

How We Work


Evaluate
Understanding your needs

The first step is to find out what is important to you; what are you trying to achieve? What are your program objectives? What are your budgets? What about timescales and other drivers? We recognise that these will differ from organisation to organisation and will vary between different sectors.



Options
Requirements specification

If you are new to the industry, it can be difficult to know exactly what you need and to explain what you want. This is also true for established broadcasters when it comes to upgrading your studios. However, we do, so why not let us help create your Requirements Specification, describing the facilities you need to make your programs, free of charge, which you are welcome to send to other suppliers.






Proposals
We produce detailed proposal

If you choose to work with us, we will produce a detailed Proposal. This will specify all equipment you need to create your project. We can also make recommendations on equipment that isn’t necessary, but you might like to have, and explain how this will integrate into the Proposal.



Design
Dotting the i’s and crossing the t’s

If you’re happy with our proposal, a project manager will be assigned to you who will work on the detailed design, dotting the i’s and crossing the t’s. This ensures there are no loose ends and that all parties are clear on what is expected from them.



Implement
Have your brand new equipment ready to use

The implementation phase deals with procurement, pre-fabrication, configuration, testing, inspection, delivery, logistics, installation, commissioning and training. You will now have your brand new equipment ready to use alongside your team who now know how to use it.



Support
Our service doesn’t stop

Our service doesn’t stop at the point of going live. Our dedicated support team are on-hand when you need help after you’ve finished with your project manager.
